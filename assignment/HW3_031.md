5.) Who have coefficient of variation be better? between body weight and heart weight

```{R}
cvBwt <- sd(cats$Bwt)/mean(cats$Bwt)
print(cvBwt)
cvHwt <- sd(cats$Hwt)/mean(cats$Hwt) 
print(cvHwt)
```
